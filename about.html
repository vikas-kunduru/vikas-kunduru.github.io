Spatial Analysis

Starting with basic manual works in this path I found myself working as a Student Intern with complex datasets and projects in a realistic approach at City of Brimbank soon. Diving into huge datasets with subtle details to project requirements and guidelines became my daily companion.


Out of this love towards digging data and making it more visually understandable, my passion for Data Modelling and visualization evolved. Turning back didn't feel right anymore and skipping the steps of automating daily tasks, quality checks became inevitable.


Experiments & Prototypes

My research work - Digital Distraction is an attempt to unveil downsides of today's technology lifestyle and showcase how millennials and later generations are affected by it.

Know your bin day is a prototype of mobile/web application that will show users their bin collection days and custom reminders based on their locality and address.

Work Management System is a prototype the helps small scale companies and teams to share, manage, and keep track of work progress.


I trust everyone's got something to learn & teach, not to limit imagination, teamwork, and not to leave anything to chance.
